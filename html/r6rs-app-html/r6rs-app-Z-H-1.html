Summary

This document contains non-normative appendices to the Revised6 Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme. These appendices contain advice for users and suggestions for implementors on issues not fit for standardization, in particular on platform-specific issues.

This document frequently refers back to the Revised6 Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme [3] and the Revised6 Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme — Libraries — [4]; references to the report are identified by designations such as “report section” or “report chapter”, and references to the library report are identified by designations such as “library section” or “library chapter”.

We intend this report to belong to the entire Scheme community, and so we grant permission to copy it in whole or in part without fee. In particular, we encourage implementors of Scheme to use this report as a starting point for manuals and other documentation, modifying it as necessary.
